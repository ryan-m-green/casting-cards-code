Our Mission

To support the shared craft of storytelling across any narrative game. For worldbuilders, this means a clear, human-centered way to understand relationships, motivations, and world dynamics. For players, it means effortless note-taking and the ability to step into the story with clarity and confidence, supported by tools that help them stay grounded in the world.
