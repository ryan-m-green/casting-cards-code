1

3

Bring players in

Invite your players to join the campaign. No subscriptions required for players so everyone can access and participate.

4

Play together

Collaborate in real-time, connect with each other through storytelling, and focus on what matters most, the story itself.

Mission & Philosophy

This exists because storytelling shouldn't be a memory test. Whether you're building worlds or just trying to keep up with the plot, you deserve tools that make it easier. GMs get organization without the headache. Players get clarity without the stress. The table gets a shared understanding of the world you're creating together.

Collaboration at the table

Tools that help storytellers and players stay aligned, share context, and build a world together.

Clarity that supports everyone

A simple, intuitive structure that reduces confusion and keeps the story moving.

Shared understanding

Features that help each person see the same world, even when their perspectives differ.

Respect for the story

Systems that highlight motivations, relationships, and consequences without overwhelming the table.

From the Creator

I'm awful at taking notes, and my dyslexia makes it even harder to keep track of story details. When I'm playing, I can focus on the moment or try to write things down, but I can't do both. My notes were scattered and incomplete, and I kept losing track of how everything fit together. I built this app to fix that for myself, and it grew into a tool meant to help storytellers and players stay organized and understand the world they're creating.

~ Ryan Green
